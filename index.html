About

Bangladesh International School & College (BISC) is an English Medium institution managed by Bangladesh Army Headquarters, Dhaka Cantonment. BISC started its journey on 14th March 1995. The college today is one of the most prestigious institutions in the country with around 2900 students from Nursery to A level & HSC. On our site, you will find much useful news and information on BISC including the Notice Board and Useful Links.
